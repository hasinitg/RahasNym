    • I am Hasini Gunasinghe from Sri Lanka. I believe happiness and peacefulness in mind as one of the most important things in life, therefore I try to stay happy and peaceful myself while trying to make my loved ones and others happy too. :)

    I am currently in the second year of PhD program in Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. My subject areas of interest are information security, cryptography and machine learning. I work as graduate research assistant and my research supervisor is Professor Elisa Bertino. I carry out research on biometrics-based authentication and digital identity management techniques.

    My passion is to contribute to human advancement with the use of technology. When combined it with my areas of interests, my goal is to develop techniques to make digital identity a secure, privacy preserving and usable thing for all. Sharing what I learn new with others is another passion which keeps me motivated. I currently do it though my blogs, during my free time.

    Before joining grad school, I worked as a Software Engineer in WSO2 Inc where I was a member of its Security and Identity Server team. I obtained my bachelors degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.
